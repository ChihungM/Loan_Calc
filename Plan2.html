About Plan 2


You fall under this category if you took out your loan (excluding Postgraduate loans):

on or after 1st September 2012 whilst in England or Wales
or you received EU funding in England or Wales.

How your repayments are calculated

Payments will be taken from your salary during any pay period if they exceed or meet the threshold before tax. Your pay period will typically be every:

- week
- two weeks
- four weeks
- calendar month

The earnings threshold is:

- £494 a week
- £2,143 a month
- £25,725 a year

Any amount you earn over the threshold, 9% will be deducted from that difference.

For example, you earn £2,500 every month before tax so the difference will be £2500 - £2,143 = £357
9% of £357 is £32.13 so your student loan repayment is £32.13 every month.
